01 — About

A little about me.

I'm a robotics engineering student at the University of Michigan, working at the crossroads of human–robot interaction, autonomous systems, and computer vision.

At the MAVRIC Lab, I focus on social robotics and how machines can better understand and respond to human presence — from gaze and pose estimation to scene-level perception.

Outside the lab, I spent three years living in Barcelona playing futsal, which taught me a lot about coordination, quick decision-making, and how much fun robots would have playing sports.
